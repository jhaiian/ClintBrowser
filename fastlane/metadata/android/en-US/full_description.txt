Clint is a free and open-source Android browser with no dedicated backend infrastructure, meaning it does not collect any of your data. It features a clean, customizable Material Design interface, the built-in **Quiver Guard** content blocker for ads and trackers, and an advanced download manager. Every feature is designed around the philosophy that your browsing data belongs to you and only you.

Privacy & Security

· Blocks 16+ trackers and analytics domains
· Third-party cookie blocking
· Generic user-agent to reduce fingerprinting
· HTTPS-Only Mode
· Incognito mode with fully isolated sessions

Browsing Features

· Multi-tab browsing with bottom sheet tab switcher
· Reader mode for distraction-free reading
· Data saver mode (blocks images, disables auto-play video)
· Speech-to-text in address bar
· Search suggestions
· Browser history
· Pull-to-refresh
· Desktop mode
· Pop-up protection
· Full-screen video support
· Content Blocker

Customization

· 3 themes: Dark, Light, Legacy
· 7 accent colors including Material You
· 3 surface intensity levels
· 3 address bar positions: Top, Bottom, Split
· 2 menu styles: Pop-up or bottom sheet
· Nested scroll controls
· Hide status bar option

Bookmarks

· Save pages with one tap
· Sort by title, last visit, or date added
· Stored locally in SQLite

Downloads & Uploads

· Upload images, videos, audio, and recordings
· Advanced download manager using OkHttp (1DM inspired)
· Custom download location
· Split download parts
· Multi-threading
· Pause and resume support
· Long-press any image to download

Site Settings

· Manage camera, microphone, location, notifications
· Configurable defaults: Ask first, always deny, always allow
· Site exceptions

Additional Features

· Local crash log viewer
· Intent handling for external apps (YouTube, Spotify, etc.)
· Favicons

Requirements

· Android 8.0 (API 26) or higher
· WebView Implementation

App permissions

INTERNET
Lets the app load websites and download files.

ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE
Lets the app check if you're connected to the internet and whether you're on Wi-Fi or mobile data, so downloads can pause when you go offline and resume when you reconnect.

WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
Lets the app save downloaded files to your Downloads folder. Only applies on Android 9 and below.

POST_NOTIFICATIONS
Lets the app show download progress, completion, and failure alerts in your notification bar and site permission.

REQUEST_INSTALL_PACKAGES
Lets the app install APK files you download directly from the download screen.

CAMERA
Used when a website asks you to upload a photo and you choose to take one with your camera, and when a website requests camera access for video calls.

RECORD_AUDIO
Used for the voice search button in the browser, and when a website requests microphone access for voice or video calls.

MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS
Required for WebRTC voice and video calls on websites to work properly. The WebView needs this to manage audio routing during a call.

ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION
Used when a website asks for your location, for example to show nearby places or get directions.

ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION
Same as ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION but used as a fallback when only approximate location is available on your device.

QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES
Lets the app detect which apps on your device can handle special links, like opening a phone number in your dialer or a store link in the Play Store.

FOREGROUND_SERVICE
Lets the app keep downloading files even when you switch to another app.

FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC
Works together with FOREGROUND_SERVICE to tell Android that the background activity is a file download.

REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS
Prevents Android from pausing downloads when the device is in battery saver or Doze mode.

WAKE_LOCK
Prevents your device from sleeping while a download is in progress so files don't get stuck halfway.

RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED
Lets the app check for unfinished downloads after your device restarts and resume them automatically.
