Clint is a free, open-source Android browser with no dedicated backend infrastructure, built with privacy in mind. It features a clean, customizable interface that lets users tailor the look and feel to their preference. It blocks known trackers and analytics, and completely isolates incognito sessions. Every feature is designed around the philosophy that your browsing data belongs to you and only you.

Privacy & Security

· Blocks 16+ trackers and analytics domains
· Zero Google telemetry
· Third-party cookie blocking
· Generic user-agent to reduce fingerprinting
· HTTPS-Only Mode
· Incognito mode with fully isolated sessions

Browsing Features

· Multi-tab browsing with bottom sheet tab switcher
· Reader mode for distraction-free reading
· Data saver mode (blocks images, disables auto-play video)
· Speech-to-text in address bar
· Search suggestions
. Browser History
· Pull-to-refresh
· Desktop mode
· Popup protection
· Full-screen video support

Customization.

· 3 themes: Default purple, Dark, Light
· 7 accent colors including Material You
· 3 surface intensity levels
· 3 address bar positions: Top, Bottom, Split
· 2 menu styles: Pop-up or Bottom Sheet
· Nested scroll controls
· Hide status bar option

Bookmarks

· Save pages with one tap
· Sort by title, last visit, or date added
· Stored locally in SQLite

Downloads & Uploads

· Custom OkHttp download engine
· Upload images, videos, audio, and recordings
· Long-press any image to download
· Real-time progress with cancel support
· Install APKs directly from downloads screen
· Automatic duplicate filename handling

Site Settings

· Manage camera, microphone, location, notifications
· Configurable defaults: Ask first, Always deny, Always allow
· Site exceptions

Additional Features

· Local crash log viewer
· Intent handling for external apps (YouTube, Spotify, etc.)
· Favicons.

Requirements

· Android 8.0 (API 26) or higher
· Android WebView (Bromite) (Android System WebView) Etc.
